Beasan
Cianjur is a city known as a producer of superior and quality rice in Indonesia. With its ecosystem and topography dominated by agricultural land, this also shapes Cianjur batik motifs. One of the most famous Cianjur batik motifs is the Beasan motif which is inspired by rice plants as one of the largest crop commodities in Cianjur.Source:
