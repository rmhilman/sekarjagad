Sidoluhur
Sidoluhur motif batik also contains a profound philosophy. Derived from the word "luhur" which means great and honorable, it is hoped that anyone who wears Sidoluhur batik can become a role model and receive honor in life. Interestingly, Sidoluhur batik is often used during "mitoni" ceremonies (traditional ceremonies when entering the 7th month of pregnancy). Behind that, Sidoluhur batik unravels the underlying happiness of human life.
