Gentongan
It is originally from Tanjung Bumi village of Madura. Gentong is a big jar made of clay. It is called Batik Gentongan because the dying process uses gentong. The natural dye is added to water in gentong and then sealed, batik maker will put the fabric in the gentong and let it for 24 hours and then repeated for months and sometimes for a year. The color is very vibrant and because of this dying process the color lasts forever.Source:
