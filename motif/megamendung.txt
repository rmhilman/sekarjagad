Megamendung
This Cirebon batik motif, a city famous for its shrimp commodity, is indeed very distinctive and easily recognizable with its clouds lined up and its elegant blue color. The Megamendung motif has a deep meaning, which is that humans should be able to hold back anger in any situation and must be wise like the cloudy clouds that can make the atmosphere cool.Source:
