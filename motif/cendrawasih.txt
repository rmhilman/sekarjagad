Cendrawasih
Cendrawasih bird or Bird of Paradise (Paradisaea), is a motif that illustrates the endemic bird from Papua land. Cendrawasih is one of the rare bird species, protected by the Indonesian government. This bird is believed as a Paradise bird that connects the earth with heaven. This motif is also considered a sacred motif and represents the identity of Papuan community both residing in Papua and West Papua land.Source:
